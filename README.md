<h1 align="center">📊 Excel Sales Team Performance Dashboard</h1>

<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>
<p>
This project presents an interactive <b>Sales Team Performance Dashboard</b> built using Microsoft Excel.
The dashboard analyzes the performance of sales executives across different regions and compares their
actual sales with assigned targets. It helps identify top-performing employees, underperforming employees,
and regional sales trends.
</p>

<h2>🎯 Problem Statement</h2>
<p>
Sales managers need a quick and effective way to evaluate the performance of their sales teams.
Manual tracking of daily sales and targets can be time-consuming and difficult to analyze.
This dashboard provides a visual and interactive solution to track sales performance and
measure how close each executive is to their assigned targets.
</p>

<h2>📂 Dataset Description</h2>

<ul>
<li>Sales Executive Name</li>
<li>Region</li>
<li>Daily Sales Data (Day 1 – Day 5)</li>
<li>Total Sales</li>
<li>Target Sales</li>
<li>Target Hit Percentage</li>
<li>Away From Target Percentage</li>
</ul>

<h2>📊 Dashboard Features</h2>

<ul>
<li>Sales performance comparison between executives</li>
<li>Regional sales analysis</li>
<li>Total sales vs target comparison</li>
<li>Target achievement percentage tracking</li>
<li>Interactive charts and Pivot Tables</li>
</ul>

<h2>🛠 Tools & Technologies Used</h2>

<ul>
<li>Microsoft Excel</li>
<li>Pivot Tables</li>
<li>Charts and Graphs</li>
<li>Conditional Formatting</li>
<li>Dashboard Design</li>
</ul>

<h2>📈 Key Insights</h2>

<ul>
<li>Identifies top-performing sales executives.</li>
<li>Highlights executives who are below their sales targets.</li>
<li>Provides a clear comparison of regional sales performance.</li>
<li>Helps managers monitor team productivity efficiently.</li>
<li>Supports data-driven decision making.</li>
</ul>
